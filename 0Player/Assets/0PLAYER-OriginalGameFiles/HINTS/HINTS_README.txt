Welcome to the Hint Directory!

0PLAYER is constructed to communicate its ideas through its puzzle design and overall structure. Nevertheless, its architects do not think themselves to be capable of true telepathy. Hints are thusly provided here for each major mechanic of the experience, in the form of screenshots of sequential gamestates.

Where provided, images within a sequence are separated, allowing the viewer to consider "what could happen next" before clicking to reveal the next hint. The final hint is a precise description of the mechanic.

Mechanics are grouped according to the regions which focus on them, numbered roughly in the order they appear within that region. Regions themselves are also roughly numbered in order of complexity / amount of prerequisite knowledge required.

Additionally, an annotated version of the game is provided both for the novelty of learning the internal names of puzzles, and for useful reference in discussions/bug reports.
